Strong Editor

Words sell. Strong words sell more.


You want to express your ideas the best possible way. It doesn’t matter whether you want to sell a product or an idea, the wording matters.


Strong Editor is an online text editor that highlights poor wording in real-time.


Strong Editor is not going to replace Google Docs or Microsoft Office as your word processor. No, that’s not the idea. The idea is that you can use Strong Editor as the first tool to type in your draft and when you follow its suggestions, your first version will already be stronger than it would be without using Strong Editor.


Strong Editor is free, fast, and privacy focused. Nothing you type here gets recorded anywhere, this website doesn’t even use any tracking cookies or other nonsense.


Simple, effective, fast and powerful.
